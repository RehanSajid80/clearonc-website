Privacy Policy

Welcome to our website ("ClearOnc.com"). ClearOnc Insights, a protected series of AccelerOnc Studio Holdings LLC ("ClearOnc," "us" or "we") provides this "Privacy Policy" to describe how we handle your personal information when you visit our website.

This Privacy Policy does not apply to your individually identifiable or protected health information subject to HIPAA.

SECURITY SAFEGUARDS

We use reasonable measures and safeguards to protect your personal information. No data management system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT

"Personal Information" is information that you provide to us about yourself on our website, such as your name, email address, phone number, and the content of messages you input into our webforms.

We also collect information automatically through cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies including IP address, location, device ID, traffic data, logs, and usage details.

You may refuse or disable cookies by adjusting your web browser settings. We do not honor Do Not Track signals at this time.

HOW WE USE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

We may use your Personal Information:

  • To communicate with you and respond to inquiries

  • For legal, security, or safety reasons
  • In connection with corporate transactions

HOW WE DISCLOSE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

We may share your Personal Information:

  • For business purposes with vendors and service providers
  • With your direction or consent
  • With affiliates within our corporate group
  • For compliance with law
  • In the context of a transaction

We do not sell any Personal Information for money or compensation.

THIRD PARTY WEBSITES AND LINKS

This Privacy Policy does not address practices of third parties. We do not control such third parties and are not responsible for their content or privacy policies.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

We do not knowingly collect Personal Information from children under 18 without parental consent.

RETENTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

We generally retain records only as long as necessary for business operations and legal requirements.

CALIFORNIA RESIDENTS
