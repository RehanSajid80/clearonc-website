About Us

About ClearOnc

ClearOnc modernizes how oncology expertise is delivered. We were built to close the gap between when cancer decisions are made and when expert insight arrives — and to ensure that expertise remains accessible across the full cancer journey.

Our Mission

To make leading cancer expertise accessible when decisions matter most — faster, simpler, and smarter.

Our Vision

A world where every cancer diagnosis leads to the right treatment — every time, regardless of geography.

Our Promise

The right expertise. Anywhere. Without the wait.

Why Moffitt

ClearOnc’s insights originate within the national leader in cancer prevention, advanced therapies, and rare cancers: Moffitt Cancer Center.

Moffitt is nationally recognized for:

  • The most innovative treatments for even the rarest and most aggressive cancers
  • Deep subspecialization across more than 50 cancer types
  • The country’s largest investment in preventative care
  • Outcomes up to 4× better in select high-acuity cancers compared to national averages

ClearOnc extends that expertise beyond physical walls — aligning the therapeutic area to the member’s specific cancer to achieve the best possible outcomes.
