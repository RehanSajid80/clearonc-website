Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

ClearOnc is an online second-opinion platform designed to bring Moffitt Cancer Center’s expertise to the world with virtual expert medical opinions. An expert physician from Moffitt will review the treatment records and provide a second opinion directly to the treating oncologist.

For Employers

We work with employers to design benefit offerings that enable wellness and prevention through expert medical opinion. The first cancer diagnosis is changed up to 40% of the time, which often results in a change to the recommended treatment plan as well.

Our per-member-per-month pricing provides peace of mind for employers who wish to offer a benefit that aligns with prevention and ensures the member has the right care plan at the right time. We follow your members through their care journey — from first diagnosis to changes in treatment and survivorship.

For Healthcare Providers
