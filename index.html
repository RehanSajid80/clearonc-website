The Challenge

Cancer Decisions Are Too Important
to Leave to Chance

Cancer is one of the most complex and costly conditions organizations manage. Yet when it matters most, expert guidance arrives too late.

Expert Review Arrives Too Late

Traditional consults take 4–6 weeks. By then, treatment paths — and costs — are already locked in.

Oncology Drives Catastrophic Cost

Represents 10–15% of total medical spend and 20–40% of catastrophic claims for self-funded employers.

Access Is Unequal

Only ~20% of patients are treated at NCI-designated cancer centers. Geography shouldn't determine decision quality.

Decisions Change With Expert Review

20–40% of second opinions result in meaningful treatment changes. 30–60% refine diagnoses affecting therapy.

A single complex cancer case can exceed $500K–$1M in annual cost. Small changes in treatment decisions can swing costs by $10K–$100K+ per case.
